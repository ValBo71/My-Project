MY HOBBIES
Hello, my name is Valentin Markov. I am 38 years old from Sofia. I graduated from the Technical University in Sofia, majoring in "Power supply and electrical equipment". I have a master's degree in "Automation of the energy system". I have not been professionally involved in programming, except for small tasks in MS Excel related to in my previous work. I have designed various electrical installations. In my work I have used "AutoCad" and purely amateur "SolidWorks". I decided to develop professionally and gain new knowledge at Telerik Academy. What led me to the academy were feedback from my IT acquaintances and that the training was 6 months. Motivation to enroll in the Quality Assurance (QA) course was the partnership of Telerik Academy with many companies in the industry. This provides excellent opportunities for professional realization after graduation.
