MY HOBBIES

Bulgarian Folklore is a magical combination of beautiful movements, elegant costumes and folk poetry, accompanied by highly spirited music. It allows us to immerse ourselves in a new world, filled with deep emotions and heartfelt experiences, in tune with the lives of the ancient Bulgarians. Through it, we learn not only about the lifestyle but also about the ideas, viewpoints, joys, sorrows and spirit of Bulgaria.

Mountain climbing is excellent physical and mental conditioning. Climbing is fantastic aerobic exercise and strengthens the back and leg muscles because of the constant movement and carrying of the heavy packs. It is also a great mental workout because climbing involves long days of 12+ hours under constant strenuous pressure. During the entire climb, constant thought and care must be given to every step because one mistake could be critical and very costly to both the individual climber as well as the team.

Sewing is seen by many as a dying art but it can be a very relaxing and rewarding hobby, as you are able to create beautiful masterpieces and repair clothes. Sewing can be used to tailor clothes, create quilts, bags or just about anything. You can even learn to decorate and personalize a variety of different items.
