Recipe: Cheesecake

The recipe for cheesecake without baking is a recipe that will appeal to everyone, even those who do not make cakes often. Cheesecake without baking is not exactly cheesecake, the real classic cheesecake must be baked. But when you don't have time to turn around the oven, the recipe for cheesecake without baking is a real salvation! And although it differs from the classic cheesecake in taste and texture, the cheesecake without baking is just as tasty and pleasant cake. In this version, the recipe contains peaches, but you can use your favorite seasonal fruits, it will still turn out great.

The first step in the recipe for cheesecake without baking with fruit is the preparation of marshmallows. Grind the biscuits, add the butter and mix with your hands until you get a dough. Spread the dough evenly on the bottom of a baking dish. The most suitable is a form for a cake with falling walls.

Using a mixer, beat the cream cheese together with the powdered sugar, vanilla and lemon juice until a homogeneous mixture is obtained. Puree half of the peaches with a blender, then add the puree to the cream cheese mixture and stir again. Pour two of the three packets of gelatin into a small bowl, add about 20-30 ml of water and mix well. Put the bowl in a pot of water on the stove and dissolve the gelatin in a water bath. Once the gelatin is completely dissolved, add it to the mixture and beat well again. Pour the prepared mixture on the marsh and leave the cheesecake without baking for 2-3 hours in the freezer or refrigerator.

The last step of the recipe for cheesecake without baking with fruit is optional - the jelly fruit layer. Once the cheesecake is firm, puree the remaining peaches, which should be 1 cup. Add a tea cup of water to the peaches, mix well, then add the third packet of gelatin, which you have previously dissolved in a water bath. Mix well with a whisk or fork and add to the cheesecake. Leave in the fridge for another hour or two.
