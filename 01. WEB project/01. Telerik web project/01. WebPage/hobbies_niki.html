Mountain biking

I started with mountain biking just five years ago. But with the time passing I became more keen in this and I started to like more extreme styles in mountain biking such as All Mountain and Enduro. I have ridden interesting trails across every high mountain in Bulgaria and even aborad - in Greece. I dream of riding in the famous bike parks in the USA and Canada or at more interesting parts of the world such as the Andes.

Hiking

In my spare time, when I am not in mood for bike riding I am also keen in hiking. It makes me feel relaxed and energized. I love seeing beatiful natural scenery and I just stop to enjoy them. No matter alone or with company, it's always fun. And in the end - you could find inspiration about troubles solving.

Craft beer

Craft is the beer made by small breweries often with specific tastes and even non-standard spices. I like tasting new kind of beers from craft breweries and support them by bying their products. And who knows maybe some day I shall try to "cook" my own craft beer.
