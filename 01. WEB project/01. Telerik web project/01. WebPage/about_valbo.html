About me

The first time I got a hold of a computer was in 1984 (IMCO-2), when I discovered programming and instantly fascinated me. However, because of my many passions I turned to design and prepress. I became one of the best in my bransh but after more than 25 years of experience I realised that the profession gave me nothing new and the routine took over me completly. The last 10 years I spent dealing mainly with Quality Check of client files. The need for the theoretical basis brought back my interest for the theoretical part of the Quality Control. This was the reason I turned to QA. I think this is a very interesting and exciting career, as a continuation of the current one. I still have a lot to learn but this has never scared me.
