"Stephanie" roll

Classic recipe, part of every Bulgarian cookbook. The recipe for "Stephanie" roll is very suitable for the moments when you are expecting more guests.

The first and most important step in the recipe for "Stephanie" roll is the preparation of the products. Boil the carrots for about 10 minutes, and in the meantime prepare the minced meat. For the preparation of "Stephanie" roll you can use both pork and beef minced meat, and it is best for the minced meat to be a mixture. While preparing the minced meat, boil two of the eggs. Place the minced meat in a large bowl. Add salt and spices, as well as two raw eggs. Soak the slices in water and after they are completely soft, crush them and also add them to the minced meat. Grate the onions, chop the parsley very finely and add them to the bowl. And you start mixing with your hands. Knead thoroughly to distribute all ingredients evenly. Set the minced meat aside.

Cut the boiled carrots and cucumbers into strips. Peel the eggs and cut them into quarters. Spread the minced meat on a towel. The minced meat should be spread in a rectangular shape with a thickness of about 1 centimeter and an approximate width close to that of the pan in which you will bake the roll (for example 35 by 25 centimeters). Arrange a row of carrots on the upper side of the wide side of the minced meat, a row of pickles under it and finally a row of boiled and quartered eggs. Do not try to wrap the roll with your hands, as the "swamp" of minced meat will break. So grab the towel at the top where the vegetables are arranged and with one movement "cover" and turn down. Repeat the movement until you roll the whole roll. Finally, press both ends of the roll to "seal" and the filling does not come out when baking.

To bake a roll "Stephanie" you need a large pan, it is best to use the pans in the oven, as the roll is quite long. Place the baking paper in the tray (this will save you the effort of washing the tray afterwards) and place the roll on it. Bake a roll of "Stephanie" in a preheated 180 degree oven for 40-45 minutes.
