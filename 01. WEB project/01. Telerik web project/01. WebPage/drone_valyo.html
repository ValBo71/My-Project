Drone
valentin.markov.a28@learn.telerikacademy.com

I love flying with my drone. The feeling is pleasant. One feels at least a little free when one rises above things and sees them from above. Flying reveals views of familiar things, but from a different angle. It allows you to see something at first glance familiar in a new light. With the drone I make short films, which I then assemble into a short video. This leaves a vivid memory of the places I visited. The drone I have is a base model, but I don't worry about accidents. The previous one was lost due to a pilot error. I also have an older drone that was blown into the sea by the wind and sank. I managed to get it out, but alas, it was damaged. There is a thrill in flying, that's why I like it and practice it whenever I have time. Well the police stopped me once to question me, but that's another story .....

Drone is a flying robot that can be remotely controlled or fly autonomously through controlled flight plans.
A drone controller works by sending a radio signal from the remote control to the drone, which tells the drone what to do.
An FPV drone is an unmanned aerial vehicle with a camera that wirelessly transmits video feed to goggles.
Cool things to do with drones is take aerial photos and videos of your hikes or can join a drone racing league.
